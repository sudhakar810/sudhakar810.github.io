Bangalore-Facts
- Good to know

Our city map of Bangalore (Indien) shows 12,866 km of streets and paths. If you wanted to walk them all, assuming you walked four kilometers an hour, eight hours a day, it would take you 402 days. And, when you need to get home there are 1,850 bus and tram stops, and subway and railway stations in Bangalore.

With a total area of 10.6 square kilometers, public green spaces and parks make up 1.5% of Bangalore’s total area, 711 square kilometers. That means each of Bangalore’s 10,839,725 residents has an average of 1 square meters.

When people in Bangalore want to go out, they are spoilt for choice; our map shows more than 3,299 cafés, restaurants, bars, ice-cream parlors, beer gardens, cinemas, nightclubs and theatres. The city also boasts more than 186 sights and monuments, and far more than 5,603 retailers. Feeling tired? Our map shows more than 489 hotels and guest houses, where you can rest.
